# Chapter 7 - Conditional Judgment

A deep comparison does not rush to pick a winner.

It asks:

- Better for what?
- Under what condition?
- At what scale?
- Over what time period?
- For whom?
- With what tradeoff?

Template:

A wins when: `[condition]`  
B wins when: `[condition]`  
A fails when: `[failure mode]`  
B fails when: `[failure mode]`  
The real difference is: `[hidden difference]`  
Final judgment: `[conditional verdict]`

Do not ask only:

**Which is better?**

Ask:

**Which is better under which condition?**
