# Chapter 8 - Final-Line Builder

A final line compresses the whole comparison into one memorable sentence.

A good final line should be:

- short
- clean
- balanced
- causal
- useful
- repeatable
- emotionally clear

## Formula Examples

A starts X. B preserves X.

> Motivation starts motion. Discipline preserves motion.

A controls X. B contains Y.

> Money controls options. Time contains life.

A transforms by X. B transforms by Y.

> Fire transforms by intensity. Water transforms by persistence.

A finds X. B chooses Y.

> Intelligence finds possibilities. Wisdom chooses consequences.

A gives X. B gives Y.

> An audience gives attention. A customer gives payment.

If the final line is weak, the comparison is not finished.
