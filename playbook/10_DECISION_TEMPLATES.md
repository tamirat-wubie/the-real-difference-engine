# Chapter 10 - Decision Templates

The engine can help people make decisions.

Examples:

- job vs business
- product vs service
- two business names
- two app ideas
- two YouTube niches
- saving vs investing
- college vs self-learning

## Decision Comparison Template

Decision: what choice is being made?  
A: option one  
B: option two  
Lens: what matters most?  
Context: who is choosing and why?  
A root  
B root  
A upside  
B upside  
A risk  
B risk  
Time horizon  
Conditional verdict  
Final recommendation  
Final line

A decision comparison must end with a usable recommendation, not only an interesting observation.
