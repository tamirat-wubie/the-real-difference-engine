# Compare Anything Playbook

A first-principle guide to revealing the real difference between any two things.

## Core Promise

Turn comparison into clear thinking, useful judgment, and memorable content.

## Method

Choose the lens.  
Reduce each side to first principles.  
Map the causal chains.  
Find the hidden similarity.  
Reveal the hidden difference.  
Make a conditional judgment.  
Compress the truth into one final line.
