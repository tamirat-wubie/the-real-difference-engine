# Chapter 3 - First-Principle Roots

After choosing the lens, reduce each side to its root.

A first-principle root answers:

**What is this thing at its most basic useful level?**

Not the dictionary definition only.

The functional root.

Example:

Motivation = emotion-triggered energy toward action.  
Discipline = rule-governed continuity of action.

## Template

`[Thing] is fundamentally: [root mechanism]`

It is powered by: `[driver]`  
It acts through: `[behavior]`  
It is limited by: `[constraint]`  
It fails when: `[failure mode]`

Do not compare labels. Compare mechanisms.
