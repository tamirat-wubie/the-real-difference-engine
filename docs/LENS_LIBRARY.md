# Lens Library

A lens is the dimension of comparison.

The same two things can reveal different truths under different lenses.

## 1. Power Lens

Question:

Which has more force, control, leverage, or influence?

Example:

Hulk vs Thanos through usable power.

Final truth:

Hulk is the greater engine. Thanos is the greater system.

## 2. Time Lens

Question:

Which wins short-term, long-term, or over repeated cycles?

Example:

Motivation vs Discipline through time.

Final truth:

Motivation starts motion. Discipline preserves motion.

## 3. Meaning Lens

Question:

What does each thing symbolize?

Example:

Fire vs Water through meaning.

Fire can symbolize intensity. Water can symbolize continuity.

## 4. Transformation Lens

Question:

How does each thing change the world?

Example:

Fire vs Language through transformation.

Final truth:

Fire transforms matter. Language transforms meaning.

## 5. Trust Lens

Question:

Which creates, stores, or destroys confidence?

Example:

Brand vs Reputation through trust memory.

Final truth:

Brand is the promise. Reputation is the receipt.

## 6. Economics Lens

Question:

Which creates, captures, stores, or transfers value?

Example:

Audience vs Customer through income reality.

Final truth:

An audience gives attention. A customer gives payment.

## 7. Survival Lens

Question:

Which persists better under pressure?

Example:

Lion vs Hyena through survival strategy.

Final truth:

The lion dominates by force. The hyena survives by coordination.

## 8. Emotion Lens

Question:

How does each regulate feeling or behavior?

Example:

Love vs Fear through human behavior.

Final truth:

Fear protects the boundary. Love expands the self.

## 9. Information Lens

Question:

How does each handle knowledge, signals, or decisions?

Example:

Search Engine vs Chatbot through information access.

Final truth:

Search retrieves information. Chatbots synthesize responses.

## 10. Failure Lens

Question:

How does each break?

Example:

Open Source vs Closed Source through failure mode.

Final truth:

Open systems can fragment. Closed systems can harden.

## Lens Template

`A vs B through lens L inside context C.`

Example:

Audience vs Customer through income reality inside creator business building.
