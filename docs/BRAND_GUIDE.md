# Brand Guide

## Public Brand

The Real Difference

## Full Platform Name

The Real Difference Engine

## Taglines

- Deep comparisons from first principles.
- Everything can be compared when the lens is clear.
- I reveal the real difference between things people compare badly.

## Voice

Simple, sharp, deep, memorable.

## Title Rule

Simple title. Deep explanation. Memorable ending.

Good:

- Discipline vs Motivation: The Real Difference
- Audience Is Not Customer
- Fire vs Language: Matter vs Meaning

Bad:

- A symbolic causal ontology of motivational continuity

## Public Language

Use:

- compare
- real difference
- root
- hidden difference
- final truth
- what to compare them by

Use "lens" after training the audience. If people do not understand it, say:

**what to compare them by**

## Memory Anchor

Every post must end with a repeatable final line.
