# Quality Scoring

Before producing a topic, score it out of 100.

## Topic Score Fields

- Attention demand: /15
- Emotional tension: /10
- Hidden-depth potential: /15
- Visual potential: /10
- Practical usefulness: /15
- Monetization potential: /15
- Low controversy safety: /10
- Low copyright safety: /5
- Research feasibility: /5

## Decision Bands

- 80-100: publish soon
- 60-79: refine lens first
- 40-59: save for later
- 0-39: reject or reshape

## Example: Audience vs Customer

- Attention demand: 11/15
- Emotional tension: 7/10
- Hidden depth: 14/15
- Visual potential: 7/10
- Practical usefulness: 15/15
- Monetization potential: 15/15
- Low controversy safety: 10/10
- Low copyright safety: 5/5
- Research feasibility: 5/5

Total: 89/100

Decision: produce immediately.
