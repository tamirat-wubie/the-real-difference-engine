# Compare Anything Playbook

## A practical guide to finding the real difference between any two things.

Most people compare things at the surface.

They compare popularity, price, emotion, appearance, strength, or opinion.

This playbook teaches a deeper method:

- choose the lens
- reduce each side to first principles
- map the causal chains
- find the hidden similarity
- reveal the hidden difference
- make a conditional judgment
- compress the truth into one memorable line
