# Failure Response Plan

## Low views across all posts

Likely issue:

- weak hooks
- poor thumbnails
- unclear format
- new account

Fix:

Hook first. Comparison second. Lens third. Final truth last.

Example:

Instead of:

Discipline vs Motivation

Use:

Why motivation keeps failing you

Then reveal the comparison inside.

## Views but no saves/shares

Likely issue:

- topic is interesting but not useful
- final line is weak
- depth is too shallow

Fix:

Strengthen practical meaning and final line.

## People debate topic but ignore method

Likely issue:

Topic is overpowering the format.

Fix:

Say:

The point is not only who wins. The point is what kind of system each side represents.

## People do not understand "lens"

Use simpler language:

What to compare them by.
