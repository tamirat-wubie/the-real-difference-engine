## Day 1 - Discipline vs Motivation

**Lens:** Behavior continuity

**Final line:** Motivation starts motion. Discipline preserves motion.

### Frames

**Frame 1:**

```text
DISCIPLINE vs MOTIVATION
People compare this wrong.
```

**Frame 2:**

```text
Real comparison:
Behavior continuity
```

**Frame 3:**

```text
Motivation:
feeling -> energy -> action
```

**Frame 4:**

```text
Discipline:
rule -> repetition -> continuity
```

**Frame 5:**

```text
Hidden difference:
Motivation depends on feeling.
Discipline survives feeling.
```

**Frame 6:**

```text
Final truth:
Motivation starts motion.
Discipline preserves motion.
```

### Voiceover

People compare discipline and motivation wrong.

The real lens is behavior continuity.

Motivation is emotion-triggered action.

You feel excited, inspired, or pressured - then you move.

But emotion changes.

Discipline is rule-preserved action.

You move because the structure tells you to move.

Motivation is powerful at the beginning.

Discipline is powerful after the beginning.

Final truth:

Motivation starts motion. Discipline preserves motion.

### Caption

Motivation starts motion. Discipline preserves motion.
